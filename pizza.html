Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

TYPES OF PIZZA

  • Cheese Pizza
  • Pepperoni Pizza
  • Greek Pizza
  • Sicilian Pizza
  • California Pizza
  • Chicago Pizza

INGREDIENTS

  • Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
  • Bread
  • Flour
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Yeast
  • Any toppings of your choice

METHOD

  1. Make the dough
  2. Proof the dough
  3. Prepare the sauce and toppings
  4. Shape the dough
  5. Bake the pizza
  6. Cool the pizza
  7. Enjoy!
